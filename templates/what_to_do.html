What to do

This app will help you practice your multiplying habilities. After being introduced to the villain you'll be facing, you'll get 10 questions that you must answer. Let's take a look at an example. By the way, don't be scared. It's only an image.

You have 20 seconds to answer each question. Failing to answer in time will make your answer count as "incorrect" (). You must type your answer inside the box ( ) and press

If your answer is correct, a will show up in the right margin of the screen. In order to beat the villain, you must get at least 6 .

Once the exercise is finished, you'll have the option to start again or to see how good you did.

Depending on how many villains you defeat in a single day, you can earn medals. There's bronze, silver, gold and the super-rare medal (only for the best)

As you continue doing exercises, the villains learn from your mistakes and try to use them against you, so your only choice is to improve your skills.

Do your best and don't give up!
